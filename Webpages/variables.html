Variables
Variables are very important in programming. They are used to store and retrieve data when needed. Making a variable is called "declaring". We can declare variables by giving them a name and value. There are also different types of variables, such as constant variables. You can alter the value of normal variables after you declare them, but you cannot do the same to constant variables.
